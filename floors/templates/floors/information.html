About this website:

The purpose of this website is to provide an easy way to navigate the Kilburn Building.

A task not so easily achieved withou using a map.

The main goal of the application is to allow the users to navigate the rooms of Kilburn and to search necessary information.

Some information about the creator:

I am a third-year student at UoM. I hope this app helps me as much as it will help other people.
